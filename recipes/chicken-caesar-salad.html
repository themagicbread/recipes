Chicken Caesar Salad

Description

The Chicken Caesar Salad is a timeless favorite that brings together the rich flavors of grilled chicken, crisp romaine lettuce, crunchy croutons, and tangy Caesar dressing. Topped with grated Parmesan, it's a delightful balance of textures and tastes, perfect for a hearty lunch or a light dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 head of romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Caesar dressing (store-bought or homemade)

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Grill or pan-sear the chicken until fully cooked, about 6-8 minutes per side. Let it rest for a few minutes, then slice into strips.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the chopped romaine lettuce, croutons, and half of the Parmesan cheese.
  3. Add the Caesar dressing and toss to coat the salad evenly.
  4. Top the salad with the sliced chicken and sprinkle the remaining Parmesan cheese over the top.
  5. Serve immediately.
